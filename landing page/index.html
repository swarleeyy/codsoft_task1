About cosmic Coffee Shop

Founded in 1990, Cosmic Coffee Shop began as a small, cozy café in the heart of downtown, quickly making a name for itself with its exceptional coffee and inviting atmosphere. Established by visionary entrepreneur John Harper, the shop set out with a mission to serve not only high-quality coffee but also to create a warm, welcoming space for the community.

In addition to its employment initiatives, Cosmic Coffee Shop is known for its charitable efforts. The shop organizes regular community outreach programs, providing meals, beverages, and essential support to those in need. Through these initiatives, Cosmic Coffee Shop brings a smile to many faces, extending its reach beyond its customers to those who require a helping hand.
